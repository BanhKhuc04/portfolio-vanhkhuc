# 📌 GitHub Pin Strategy: Maximizing Impact

To "sell" your professional brand in 10 seconds, your GitHub Pinned Repositories should tell a cohesive story of Fullstack mastery and AI integration.

### Proposed 6 Pins (Order of Impact)

1.  **Portfolio HQ** (`pofolio-vanhkhuc`)
    *   **Description**: 🛸 The Command Center. Next.js 15 + AI Analyst + Fullstack CMS.
    *   **Why**: Shows the "Big Picture" and your ability to orchestrate complex systems.
2.  **V-Short SaaS Demo** (Inside `pofolio-vanhkhuc` or standalone)
    *   **Description**: ⚡ Premium URL Shortener with real-time analytics. High-performance Edge redirections.
    *   **Why**: Demonstrates Product-thinking and scalability.
3.  **AI Recruiter Agent**
    *   **Description**: 🤖 Context-aware LLM agent built with Vercel AI SDK. RAG-database integration.
    *   **Why**: Proves AI/LLM engineering capabilities.
4.  **[Best Academic Project]** (e.g., Pyneat if it's a core project)
    *   **Description**: 🧠 [Brief one-liner on the logic/impact].
    *   **Why**: Shows foundational computer science or specific niche skills.
5.  **[Open Source Contribution or Utility]**
    *   **Description**: 🛠️ [Tool name] - Solving X problem for developers.
    *   **Why**: Shows community involvement and empathy for other developers.
6.  **AI_OS Framework** (Documentation Repo)
    *   **Description**: 🛰️ Personal Brand Orchestration Framework. 100% Autonomous.
    *   **Why**: Unique meta-project showing organization and advanced AI collaboration.

---

### Tips for Premium Pins

- **Topics**: Ensure every repo has relevant topics (e.g., `nextjs`, `typescript`, `prisma`, `ai`).
- **Description**: Use Emojis to make them pop!
- **Social Preview**: Add a custom Open Graph image to each repo (Settings -> General -> Social preview).
